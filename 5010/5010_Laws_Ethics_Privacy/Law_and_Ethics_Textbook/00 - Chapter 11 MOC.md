# 📘 Chapter 11 — Legal Issues and Ethics (Map of Content)

> **Purpose:** This chapter explores the intersection of law, ethics, and computer security. It covers intellectual property protection, information as a legal object, employee/employer rights, software liability, computer crime, and the ethical frameworks that guide decision-making when the law is insufficient or unclear.
