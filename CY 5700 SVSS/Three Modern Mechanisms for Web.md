


## SOP :
 - JS attached with one origin cannot access contents from a different origin.
 - applies only to scripts.



## CSP:
- It is like SOP, but for everything other than Scripts.
- The CSP header enforces the rules or policies.
  
  
  

## CORS: 
- Allows other origins to access the resource .
- It applies the Principle of Least Privilege.
