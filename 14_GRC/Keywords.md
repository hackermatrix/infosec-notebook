
### Types of Risk Assessments 

1. **Point in time (SOC-2 Type 1)** : Refers to a **specific moment** in time, like “as of 2:00 PM on March 25, 2026.”
2. **Over the period (SOC-2 Type 2 )** : You sample the time period .



## Areas of evaluation for anything : 
- Security 
- Availability
- Process integrity 
- Confidentiality 
- Privacy of data
